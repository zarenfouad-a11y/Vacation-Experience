Picture Perfect Trip

Pier 35 • New York City • East River Views

Where would you go?

For my vacation, I went to Pier 35 in New York City, a beautiful spot along the East River with a modern park and an incredible skyline. From the pier you can see the Manhattan Bridge, the Brooklyn Bridge, and sparkling city views. I spent time enjoying the water, relaxing on benches, and breathing the fresh air. Pier 35 felt peaceful yet lively—a perfect place for sightseeing and unwinding.

What would you do?

I kept the trip active and fun. I played basketball near the pier and went to the gym for a good workout. I visited a secret scenic spot with peaceful bridge and river views, then spent time in a nearby park to sit, talk, and play football with friends. Here’s my quick activity list:

  • Play basketball by the pier
  • Exercise at the gym
  • Visit a secret scenic viewing point
  • Relax at the park and play football

What would you eat?

After walking and sports, I was excited for food. I had a tasty Texas-style chicken & burger sandwich—flavorful and filling. New York brings together foods from all over the U.S. and the world, so grabbing this bold, comforting sandwich felt just right. Eating in the park with friends made the day feel cozy and memorable.
